About the Program

FutureSkills PRIME (Programme for Re-skilling / Up-skilling of IT Manpower for Employability) is an industry-focused scheme sponsored by the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), Government of India, with the goal to build skills in 10 emerging technologies in Information Technology.

NIELIT Chandigarh serves as a Co-Lead Resource Centre for Big Data Analytics and Augmented & Virtual Reality. www.futureskillsprime.in

Role of NIELIT Chandigarh

As a Co-Lead Resource Centre for Big Data & Data Science and AR/VR

📊 Big Data & Data Science

  • Curriculum Development – Designing industry-relevant training modules from foundational data skills to advanced analytics and machine learning
  • Capacity Building – Training students, professionals, and government officials to extract insights from complex datasets
  • Hands-On Exposure – Lab-based, project-driven learning using Python, Hadoop, Spark, and TensorFlow

🎮 Augmented & Virtual Reality

  • Skill Development – Practical skills in immersive technologies: 3D modeling, Unity/Unreal Engine, XR development
  • Application-Oriented Training – Real-world applications in education, healthcare, industry, and governance
  • Innovation Enablement – Promoting interactive, immersive experiences that drive digital innovation

Flagship Programs

Government Officials Training (GoT) – Custom-tailored programs to upskill employees from Central and State Government departments, PSUs, and Autonomous Bodies, with the objective of building technical competence for improved public service delivery and digital governance.

Bootcamps – Intensive, outcome-oriented programs for students and aspiring professionals, delivered in collaboration with C-DAC under MeitY's guidance, with emphasis on job-ready skills and practical, project-based learning.

About FutureSkills PRIME

An industry-aligned skilling initiative building competencies in 10 emerging technologies

FutureSkills PRIME (Programme for Re-skilling/Up-skilling of IT Manpower for Employability) is an industry-aligned skilling initiative sponsored by the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), Government of India. The program is designed to build digital competencies in 10 emerging technologies and enhance the employability of IT professionals and government officials.

🏆 Ranked 3rd among 47 global digital skilling initiatives in the European Commission's 2024 Pact for Skills Report

10 Emerging Technologies

🤖Artificial Intelligence
📊Big Data & Data Science
🎮Augmented & Virtual Reality
🔒Cyber Security
☁Cloud Computing
🔗Blockchain Technology
📱Internet of Things
👾Robotics Process Automation
📸3D Printing / Additive Mfg
📲Social & Mobile

Courses Conducted Under the Scheme

GoT Advanced

Government Official Training – Advanced (GOT-A)

A specialized high-level technical training program tailored for public sector employees.

  • Core concepts and advanced modules
  • Cutting-edge tools and frameworks
  • Focus on Big Data, AI, Blockchain, AR/VR, Cybersecurity
  • Conducted by C-DAC & NIELIT under MeitY
GoT Basic

Government Official Training – Basic (GOT-B)

A foundational skilling initiative providing insights into fundamentals of emerging technologies.

  • Technical literacy and digital adaptability
  • 10 emerging technology domains covered
  • Designed for government officials
  • Conducted by C-DAC & NIELIT under MeitY
Bootcamp

Bootcamps

Intensive, outcome-oriented skilling programs for professionals and learners.

  • Hands-on, practical, industry-relevant
  • Led by C-DAC & NIELIT under MeitY
  • Job-ready skills focus
  • Project-based learning

Key Objectives

⚡ Reskilling & Upskilling

Enhance digital competencies of IT professionals and government officials in line with industry demands.

🎯 Emerging Technologies

Build expertise in 10 key technology domains including AI, Big Data, Cybersecurity, Blockchain, and more.

🏛 Empower Government

Enable government officials to adopt cutting-edge technologies in governance and public service delivery.

💼 Industry-Relevant

Align curriculum with industry standards to bridge the skill gap and improve employability.

📖 Lifelong Learning

Foster continuous learning through modular, self-paced, and hybrid learning formats.

🌐 National Infrastructure

Leverage a nationwide network of resource centres and virtual labs for accessible, high-quality training.

🔧 Hands-On Learning

Emphasize experiential learning through real-world projects, labs, and use-case-driven sessions.

🤝 Public-Private Collaboration

Create a collaborative ecosystem involving academia, industry, and government for sustainable skill development.

Target Beneficiaries

Government Officials Students & Career Aspirants Working Professionals PSU Employees
